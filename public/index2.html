Spend Attention WISELY

Our brains have a limited amount of attentional resources. Current helps you to understand how can you optimize your brain capacity to become more efficient and more productive in your workplace

Conquer Vigilance Decrement

After a while, people begin to lose focus and performance on the task declines. Researchers believe that this "vigilance decrement," as it is described, is the result of a drop in one's "attentional resources"

Love Your Work

Working people achieve the flow experience (deep concentration, high and balanced challenges and skills, a sense of control and satisfaction) about four times as often on their jobs, proportionately, as they do during leisure activities

Love Your Life

"People who learn to enjoy their work, who do not waste their free time, end up feeling that their lives as a whole have become much more worthwhile." -Michaly Csikszentmihalyi, #1 Flow Psychologist
